# Hashes Have The Following Types :
1. md5 ---> 32 characters
2. sha1 ---> 40 characters
3. sha256 ---> 80 characters
4. sha512 ---> 112 to 128 characters
5. bcrypt ---> 60 characters
6. handmade hash ---> <mark style="background: #FF5582A6;">high security</mark>
